About Me ~ the person behind the work

who i am

The Creative

I'm a fashion designer with a deep love for visual storytelling. I find beauty in textures, silhouettes, and the way colors speak to each other — channeling that into every project I touch.

what i do

The Craft

From concept sketches to finished garments, I work across the full design pipeline — draping, pattern-making, editorial styling, and digital illustration. Fashion is my first language.

Pattern Making Draping Illustration Styling
my vibe

The Aesthetic

Drawn to the romantic, the unexpected, and the wearable-art intersection. Think: deconstructed silhouettes with delicate embroidery, or street-meets-couture energy — always a little surprising.

beyond the studio

The Human

When I'm not designing, I'm thrifting for hidden gems, watching runway shows, dancing. Every object tells a story — I just listen carefully.

✦ Project 02

Ethereal Poise

editorial fashion

The Essence of Modern Grace

Ethereal Poise is a draping project that explores the harmonious interplay of fluidity and quiet luxury through a pastel green gown. Inspired by the softness of nature—such as water ripples and the graceful movement of swans—the garment uses sheer overlays and delicate, layered fabric to create a dreamlike, ethereal quality that evolves with every step. The silhouette is sculpted yet effortless, designed to enhance the natural form while maintaining comfort and freedom of movement. Accented with a silver metallic star brooch to provide a touch of modern glamour, the final piece represents understated opulence. It is specifically crafted for high-end social settings, including fashion exhibitions, art shows, and luxury brand launches.

✦ Project 03

Golden Reverie

sustainable fashion

Rooted in Slow Fashion

This project is basically a full-on fashion journey—from mood boards and dreamy inspirations to “okay, let’s make this real.” It starts with building a vibe through theme, forecast, and client boards, then dives into fabrics and trims to get the feel just right. The real fun? Turning all that into actual looks—illustrated front and back, with all the details that make them come alive. Think of it as taking a tiny spark of an idea and styling it all the way into a full-fledged collection moment.

✦ Project 04

Frostline

alpine edge

Beating the Winter Blues

This project is all about chasing snow in style—designing a high-performance snowboarding outerwear collection that doesn’t compromise on looks. With a deep dive into cozy-yet-tough materials like softshell, fleece, and insulation, the focus is on staying warm, moving freely, and braving the chill. The final lineup brings together bold silhouettes, smart layering, and detailed designs—made for carving through snow and turning heads while doing it.
